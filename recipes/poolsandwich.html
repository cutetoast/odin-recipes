Pool Sandwich

Description

This pool sandwich recipe is a refreshing and delicious option for summer gatherings, featuring layers of fresh ingredients and a zesty dressing.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Turkey slices
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Avocado
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and prepare your workspace.
  2. Spread mayonnaise and mustard on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Layer turkey, cheese, lettuce, tomato, and avocado on one slice of bread.
  4. Top with another slice of bread, mayo side down.
  5. Cut the sandwich in half and serve immediately.
