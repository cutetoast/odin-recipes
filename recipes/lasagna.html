Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and a rich meat sauce. It's perfect for family gatherings and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, sauté onion and garlic until translucent, then add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and Italian seasoning, and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and meat sauce. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes, then let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
